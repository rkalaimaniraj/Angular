Steering

Rack and pinion steering is a kind of mechanical steering that makes use of a gear system to convert rotational motion from the steering wheel into linear motion of the wheels. Rack and pinion steering is one of the most used types of mechanical steering. The Toyota Supra, which is a high-performance sports car, has this kind of steering since it was designed for that vehicle.
